2. Description of Service

Learn Bulgarian: Speak BG is a mobile language learning application for iOS and Android that teaches the Bulgarian language through:

  • Immersive scenario-based lessons organized by CEFR level (A1–C2)
  • Interactive exercises including multiple-choice, listening, and speaking practice
  • Spaced repetition smart review sessions
  • Gamification elements: daily streaks, energy system, coins, and a companion guide
  • Optional premium subscription for unlimited access

All lesson content is bundled within the App. An internet connection is required only for speaking exercises (speech recognition) and subscription management.
